The Seventy Nations

Humanity United in Purpose

A global movement bringing together people of every nation, culture, and background in the pursuit of moral responsibility, human dignity, spiritual understanding, and a shared future.

Inspired by the ancient vision of the nations gathering in Jerusalem, The Seventy Nations seeks to create spaces for dialogue, education, cooperation, and collective responsibility in service to humanity.

Why The Seventy Nations?

Humanity has never been more connected. Yet division, conflict, isolation, and confusion continue to separate people from one another.

The Seventy Nations exists to cultivate a global community rooted in shared values, mutual respect, moral responsibility, and the recognition of our common humanity.

We believe the future requires more than agreement. It requires understanding.

Why Jerusalem?

For thousands of years Jerusalem has inspired humanity's deepest questions about meaning, purpose, identity, and our relationship to one another.

The Seventy Nations envisions Jerusalem as a gathering place where people from every nation may learn, dialogue, celebrate, and contribute to a future grounded in wisdom and shared responsibility.

Our Four Pillars

The Jerusalem Unity Center

A Future Gathering Place for Humanity

The Jerusalem Unity Center is the educational and experiential heart of The Seventy Nations. Designed as a Center of Consciousness and Learning, it will welcome visitors from around the world to explore moral development, spiritual inquiry, dialogue, leadership, culture, and human flourishing.
